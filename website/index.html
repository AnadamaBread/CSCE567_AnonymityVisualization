Anonymity and Security amongst Adults Visualized in Tableau

Developed by Luis Baez for CSCE 567

Description

The following visualizations contain information regarding individual responses on various Anonymity, Internet Privacy, and Internet Security questions. The data comes from a July 2013 data survey of different demographics of American individuals and their practice of Internet Privacy, Security, and Anonymity. Visualizations found on this site are broken into different separate pages and can be navigated to using the buttons below. Each visualization compares the responses to the survey questions to the demographics of the surveyed individuals.
